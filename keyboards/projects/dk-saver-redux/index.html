DK Saver Redux

A replacement PCB for the DK Saver keyboard

(Photo courtesy of Lightning)

General Info​

  • AT90USB646 MCU
  • A.87B/h87/skinny A.87/TX87 footprint compatible with no TGR o-ring cutouts
  • Cherry MX Lock in-switch indicator LED compatibility on caps & scroll lock
    • The circular pad for the LEDs is positive, while the square pads are negative
  • Physical slide switch for changing the exposed indicator LED to either function as a caps lock indicator, or utilize QMK backlight
  • Unified Daughterboard w/ Molex Pico-EZmate (JST-SH connectors are too tall to properly fit lmao sorry) compatibility for easy testing & flashing without soldering a USB 2.0 cable

Cable Wiring​

USB 2.0​

Pin nameWire color
VCCRed or orange
D-White or gold
D+Green
GNDBlack or blue

(Underlined colors are the most common)

  • The USB cable shielding (usually some sort of metallic foil) surrounding the 4 main wires in your USB 2.0 cable, or the drain wire (an exposed, bare metal wire touching/connected to that foil shielding; some cables may or may not have this) should ideally be soldered to the SHIELD pin as well
    • This is technically optional, your keyboard will still work if you don't do this, but let's be real, you might as well spend the extra 5 minutes doing this if you even have one of these in the first place, don't be lazy lmfao

USB 2.0 using a PS/2 or DIN cable​

  • QMK doesn't support PS/2 keyboard output, and neither does this MCU, so you'll have to cut off the existing cable end and solder a USB connector onto that
  • You can probably figure out how to do this yourself using the magic of the internet and that USB 2.0 wiring diagram above :)
  • The same rules with soldering to the SHIELD pin from the section above apply here as well
  • I am not responsible if you fuck up your PCB from doing this incorrectly

Binaries​

All binaries have VIA enabled, with native VIA detection soon™

  • Default: dk_saver_redux.hex
  • asym_eager_defer_pk debounce (GAMING): dk_saver_redux_gaming.hex
  • Flashing backlight when typing: dk_saver_redux_backlight.hex
  • Flashing caps lock indicator when typing: dk_saver_redux_caps_lock.hex
  • Flashing scroll lock indicator when typing: dk_saver_redux_scroll_lock.hex
